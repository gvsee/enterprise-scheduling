How predicted demand is calculated
1
Historical baseline
8-week rolling average of actual appointments, broken down by day, specialty, and visit type.
2
Seasonal adjustment
Year-over-year patterns applied — e.g. flu season spikes GP demand 30% in Nov–Jan.
3
Panel growth
New patient registrations and provider panel changes adjust the baseline up or down.
4
Gap signal
Waitlist volume and missed appointment rates signal unmet demand that the model adds to the forecast.
